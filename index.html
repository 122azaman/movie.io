Avengers: Endgame

April 26, 2019

Plot

The Avengers prepare to fight the last battle against Thanos in Aveners: Endgame. Many of the
Avengers and other civilians have gone missing for 5 years due to Thanos collecting all the infinity
stones in the pervious movie. They go through a series of twists and turns in order to restore the
missing people and bring them home. They went through several losses and heartbreaks which lead to
the eventual end of the war and the Marvel Avengers in the last movie, Avengers: Endgame
